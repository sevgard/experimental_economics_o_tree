Here is a short summary of the instructions:
You are a member of a group of 4 people. Each member has an endowment of 200 points. You can put these 200 points on a private account or you can invest them fully or partially in a project. Each point you do not invest in the project will be automatically transferred to your private account.
The individual share from the mutual project is 0.5 * sum of all investments in the project.
Your total earnings therefore are given as follows:

Earnings = (200 - investment in project) + 0.5 * sum of all investments in the project

You can use the calculator to try out different scenarios.

Here you make only one type of investment decisions, the conditional investments. This investment will be payoff-relevant for you if your (randomly determined) player role is conditional.
